Our Story

More Than a
Cafe

Tucked inside the heart of Patia, Blueberrys Cafe has been Bhubaneswar's most beloved third space since 2016. We built this place with a simple idea — that great coffee, honest food, and good company are all you really need.

From early-morning cappuccinos to late-night karaoke sessions, every corner of this cafe is designed to make you feel at home. Come as you are. Stay as long as you like.
